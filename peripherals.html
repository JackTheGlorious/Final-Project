Our Products

The Glorious GameCube Controller is our most popular product and for good reason. The controller's modules are compltely customizable. We do resistance, notch, and paddle customization. The wrap is also customizable.

The Glorious Xbox Controller is the perfect controller to up your game on console or pc. We offer customization options such as wraps, grips, and custom sticks. We can even offer hardware changes such as paddles, wire/bluetooth integration, and even custom buttons.

The Glorious Mouse can help optimize your pc movement. With full RGB integration and customization. We can add up to 12 aditional buttons for optimal space usage. The DPI, cord length, and wheel type can be decieded upon.

The Glorious Stick is the Ultimate joystick for those looking for that arcade feel. The joystick, buttons, and look are fully customizable. Buttons can be moved arranged and we can even do all button setups.
